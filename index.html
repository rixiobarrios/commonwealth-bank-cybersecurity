Tips for Financial Cybersecurity

Here are some tips for financial cybersecurity: Use strong passwords:

  • Use different passwords for each account and store them in a secure location.
  • Use multi-factor authentication: Enable multi-factor authentication.
  • Avoid suspicious messages and links: Be suspicious of unsolicited emails and phone calls.
  • Protect your computer: Understand internet safety features and protect your computer.
  • Keep your devices and apps up-to-date: Keep your devices and apps up-to-date.
  • Monitor your credit card transactions: Sign up for fraud alerts with your credit card providers and monitor your credit card transactions for suspicious activity.

Other tips for financial cybersecurity include:

  • Researching before you buy
  • Watching what you click
  • Controlling access to data and systems
  • Using security software
  • Putting up a firewall
